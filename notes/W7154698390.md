## TL;DR

Vegetation greening in semi-arid regions triggers energy-mediated feedbacks that increase precipitation efficiency and offset expected reductions in water yield.

- **Problem:** Vegetation greening in semi-arid regions is expected to increase evapotranspiration and reduce water yield, yet observed streamflow trends often do not match this expectation — the role of greening-induced energy feedbacks on precipitation recycling is poorly understood.
